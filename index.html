Lava Lands of central Oregon

Newberry Volcano

The largest volcano in the Cascade Range of Oregon, Newberry Volcano is both seismically and geothermically active. Its last eruption 1,300 years ago deposited the Big Obsidian Flow. Newberry's beautiful twin lakes developed within the caldera created after an eruption that coincided with the onset of the last glacial period, approximately 75,000 years ago.
